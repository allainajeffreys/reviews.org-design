Do

  • Keep clear space of at least the mark's width on every side.
  • Pick the variant that maximizes contrast with the background.
  • Use the navy-circle icon for avatars and favicons.

Don't

  • Recolor, add effects, or place the red mark on a clashing fill.
  • Stretch, rotate, or crop the mark or wordmark.
  • Overuse the doodle variant — it's an occasional wink, not the default.
Writing style

Brand voice

Conversational, direct, and straightforward — written to earn reader trust, not to sound like a brochure. Four dials set the default tone; pieces can dial in and out within range.

Mobile-first

Scannability rules
